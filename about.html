HOW TO PLAY ?

This game is not like other games, it is a roleplay game that requires a lot of effort but gives you a super experience with your friends as a result.

Your goal is to activate the fuses at the restricted energy level using the control panel in your hand. The system can sometimes defeat you, but don't worry, if you have the chance, you can activate them all. Using the REBOOT button, all fuses can be activated with a 1/5 chance.

PREPARATION

Minimum : 3 PEOPLE

Maximum : 10 PEOPLE

3 PEOPLE: "1 RED EYE" AND "2 ESCAPER"

4 PEOPLE: "1 RED EYE" AND "3 ESCAPER"

5 PEOPLE: "2 RED EYE" AND "3 ESCAPER"

6 PEOPLE: 2 / 4

7 PEOPLE: 3 / 4

8 PEOPLE: 3 / 5

9 PEOPLE: 3 / 6

10 PEOPLE: 3 / 7

MAX RED EYE = 3

MIN RED EYE = 1

DEEP TOPIC

-First, choose a WIDE AREA and set FIVE REGIONS.

-Then choose your character according to the above article.

For example, 1 of 3 friends is RED EYE and the other 2 are ESCAPER. RED EYE's mission is to catch the other 2 people, the other two people's mission is to OPEN 5 FUSES.

RULE

THE SELECTED REGION MUST BE IN ORDER TO FIT THE SYSTEM

FUSE 1 : FIELD 1

FUSE 2 : FIELD 2

FUSE 3 : FIELD 3

FUSE 4 : FIELD 4

FUSE 5 : FIELD 5

-START PLACE : MAIN PLACE

-YOU CAN ONLY USE THE "REBOOT" BUTTON ON START PLACE

-THE ESCAPERS MUST IN A GROUP WITHOUT ON DEVICE
